About HEART

HEART Technology Park represents Vietnam's most ambitious leap into the future of artificial intelligence and technological innovation. Situated in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City, our 50-hectare campus is designed to be the epicenter of AI research, development, and commercialization in Southeast Asia.

Our Vision

To establish Vietnam as a global leader in artificial intelligence research and development, fostering innovation that benefits humanity while driving economic growth and technological advancement across the region.

Our Mission

We are committed to creating an ecosystem where brilliant minds collaborate to solve the world's most challenging problems through AI innovation. Our mission encompasses research excellence, talent development, and the creation of breakthrough technologies that will shape the future of industries worldwide.

Innovation Ecosystem

HEART Technology Park brings together leading researchers, innovative startups, established corporations, and venture capital in a dynamic environment designed for collaboration and breakthrough innovation. Our facilities include state-of-the-art research laboratories, modern office spaces, and cutting-edge technology infrastructure.

Global Impact

With partnerships spanning across major technology hubs in Silicon Valley, London, Tokyo, and Singapore, HEART serves as a bridge connecting Vietnamese innovation with global markets and opportunities.

Research Excellence

Our research programs focus on artificial intelligence, machine learning, robotics, and emerging technologies that will shape the future of human-computer interaction and automation.

Sustainable Development

We are committed to developing AI solutions that address global challenges including climate change, healthcare accessibility, education equality, and sustainable economic growth.

Community Engagement

HEART Technology Park actively engages with local communities, educational institutions, and government agencies to ensure that AI development benefits all members of society.

Future Vision

Looking ahead, we envision HEART Technology Park as the cornerstone of Vietnam's transformation into a global AI powerhouse, leading innovations that will benefit humanity for generations to come.

Educational Programs

Our comprehensive educational programs include workshops, seminars, internships, and research opportunities designed to nurture the next generation of AI professionals and researchers.

Industry Collaboration

We foster deep collaboration between academia, industry, and government to ensure that AI research translates into practical solutions that benefit society and drive economic growth.

Organization Structure

HEART Technology Park operates under a Public-Private Partnership (PPP) model, combining government oversight with private sector efficiency. Our organizational structure is designed to maximize innovation, ensure regulatory compliance, and deliver world-class infrastructure and services.
